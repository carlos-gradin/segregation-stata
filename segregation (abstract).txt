TITLE

    'SEGREGATION': module to compute segregation indices 
    (two-group or multigroup; individual or aggregate data).

DESCRIPTION/AUTHOR(S)

   dicseg estimates segregation across units (e.g.  
   occupations) in a two-group context. localseg estimates multigroup 
   segregation (Alonso-Villar and Del Rio, 2010). 


      KW: segregation
      KW: indices
      KW: groups
      KW: race
